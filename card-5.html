Queer Geographies

This place
is famous,
Salmacis.
I went to take
the waters.
I faced
the statue
where I washed,
Naked
among others.
Some had
all our parts,
Or drank copiously
from the spring
And wished;
the bottom of
the pool
Is littered
with silver too.

Mcgrann, Molly. “Hermaphroditus.” Vol. 7, no. 3, 2000, pp. 109–114., www.jstor.org.proxy.seattleu.edu/stable/20163773. Accessed 12 Dec. 2019.
